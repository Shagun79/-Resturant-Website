1. Gordon Ramsay

Surprise, surprise – to no one, Gordon Ramsay is the most popular
chef in the world. The ultra-famous chef and TV personality
is probably the most well-known celebrity chef ever. His career includes a total of 16 (!) Michelin stars, numerous awards, and a long list of popular TV shows, including Hell’s Kitchen, MasterChef US, and Kitchen Nightmares, which have inspired endless memes. Ramsay is well-known for his cooking talent,
his on-screen charisma,
and his fiery temper – there is a lot of beeping going on whenever he makes a TV appearance, that’s all we’re going to say.

2. Guy Fieri

According to the number of searches on Google, Guy Fieri is the second most popular chef in the world in 2022. AD The Emmy-award-winning American restaurateur is one of the most beloved TV personalities in the biz – and one of the most prolific. He’s featured in various cooking programs on the Food Network, such as Guy’s Big Bite, Diners, Drive-Ins and Dives, and Minute to Win It. Besides his television work, Fieri has written several cookbooks and reportedly owns more than 70 restaurants and 14 brands. And while he does not have a Michelin star to his name, Fieri has other accolades to be proud of.
